Study Information Sheet

You are being invited to participate in a research study titled Indian Agriculture Wage Survey. This study is being done by Steven Brownstone from UC San Diego. You were selected to participate in this study because you live in a village of interest.

The purpose of this research study is to understand how wages for agricultural labor vary across villages. Your participation in this research should last approximately 20 minutes. If you agree to take part in this study, you will be asked to answer some questions about agriculture in your village.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you can withdraw at any time. Choosing not to participate or withdrawing will result in no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are entitled. You are free to skip any question that you choose.

If you have questions about this project or if you have a research-related problem, you may contact the researcher(s), Steven Brownstone +91 9667990639. If you have any questions concerning your rights as a research subject, you may contact the UC San Diego Office of IRB Administration at irb@ucsd.edu or 858-246-4777.

By participating in this research you are indicating that you are at least 18 years old, have read this consent form, and agree to participate in this research study. Please keep this consent form for your records.
