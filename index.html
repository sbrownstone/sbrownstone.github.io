Backgound

I am a sith-year PhD candidate in economics at the University of California San Diego. My research is in development economics, agricultural economics, and political economy. I partner with CEGIS and J-PAL South Asia for my projects in India. I have a bachelor's degree from Brown University, where I majored in Math-Economics. Before starting at Brown, I worked at IDinsight. First, as an associate in Lusaka, Zambia and then as a Junior Economist in New Delhi, India
